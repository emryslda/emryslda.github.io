My name is Ludovico,
<p></p>
Currently I am a PhD at <a href="http://www.lisa.u-pec.fr/fr" target="_blank">Laboratoire Interuniversitaire des Systèmes Atmosphériques</a> (LISA) in Paris.
<p></p>

I graduated in Atmospheric Science and Technology <a href="http://www.dsfc.univaq.it/it/corso-magistrale-lmast.html" target="_blank"></a> (Physics)
at the <a href="http://www.dsfc.univaq.it/it/corso-magistrale-lmast.html" target="_blank">University of Roma Sapienza and L'Aquila</a>   (joint-university degree)
in October 2020.
<p></p>

Since I was a child I have been attracted to nature. I grew up in one of the greenest regions of Italy and Europe (Abruzzo) that can offer beautiful landscapes (from mountain to sea), very good wine and food. The interest in protecting the environment, preserving the air quality and a deep passion for hiking, led me to choose to study meteorology and climatology.
<p></p>


<p style="text-align: justify;">
I am a member of different organizations involved in sharing Atmospheric Science: <a href="https://www.aisam.eu/" target="_blank">AISAM</a> (Associazione Italiana di Scienze dell'Atmosfera e Meteorologia), <a href="http://www.meteoaquilano.it/" target="_blank">MeteoAQuilano</a> and <a href="https://www.aivulc.it/it/" target="_blank">AIV</a> (Associazione Italiana di Vulcanologia).
