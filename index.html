Welcome

My name is Ludovico,

I am a Postdoctoral researcher at Laboratoire Atmosphères, Observations Spatiales (LATMOS) in Paris. I am currently working on modeling the aerosol-cloud interactions within the European project CERTAINTY (Cloud-aERosol inTeractions & their impActs IN The earth sYstem)

I got a PhD at Laboratoire Interuniversitaire des Systèmes Atmosphériques (LISA) on a thesis entitled "Propriétés, distribution et effet radiatif des aérosols en Ile de France par une approche couplée mesure-modélisation" in the framework of the Atmospheric ChemistRy Of the Suburban foreSt (ACROSS) project

I graduated in Atmospheric Science and Technology (Physics) at the University of Roma Sapienza and L’Aquila (joint-university degree) in October 2020.

Since I was a child I have been attracted to nature. I grew up in one of the greenest regions of Italy and Europe (Abruzzo) that can offer beautiful landscapes (from mountain to sea), very good wine and food. The interest in protecting the environment, preserving the air quality and a deep passion for hiking, led me to choose to study meteorology and climatology
